Kit Options

We have a wide variety of Amigurumi kits to choose from! Though our kits are labeled with different skill levels, there is no need to limit yourself. Our designs have different methods based on the kits choosen and we encourage everyone to be creative and explore!

Skill level What to expect Designs Average Price
Beginner Our starter kit. Includes two needles of different sizes, a bundle of yarn, and an instructional pamphlet on basic corcheting techniques as well as one for the choosen design. Small. Design choices will be more simple, as they will focus more on building up your skills and confidence. $25
Intermidiate For those who know the basics. This kit will include the recommended needle for the selected design, two different colored bundles of yarn, and an instructional pamphlet. Medium. Designs will have more intericate details and techniques. For those who wish to build more experience and skills. $35
Advanced For those who know what to do but don't want to gather the materials. This kit will include, the recommended needle for the choosen design, three bundles of different colored yarn, and an instructional pamphlet. Large. Designs will have a guide for those who want them but overall this is a chance for you to get creative! $45
